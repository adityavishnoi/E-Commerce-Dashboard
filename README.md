# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project is an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional insights. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that help businesses monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

The primary objective of this project is to provide stakeholders with a comprehensive view of business performance through an intuitive and interactive reporting experience.

# Dashboard Features

## Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance using KPI cards including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Unit Price
* Total Customers
* Year-over-Year Growth

This page enables management to quickly evaluate overall business performance.

---

## Sales Trend Analysis

Visualizes monthly sales trends to identify:

* Seasonal demand
* Sales fluctuations
* Growth patterns
* Monthly performance comparison

This helps businesses understand when sales increase or decline throughout the year.

---

## Product Performance

Analyze products based on:

* Sales Quantity
* Revenue Contribution
* Best Selling Products
* Product Ranking

The dashboard highlights products such as:

* Pack
* PK
* Tubs
* Rolls
* Tins

allowing businesses to identify their most successful product categories.

---

## Customer Insights

The customer analysis section provides insights into:

* Total Customers
* Top Customers
* Customer Distribution

* Customer Contribution to Revenue

These insights help businesses understand customer value and improve retention strategies.

## Location Analysis

The geographical analysis allows users to explore sales performance across different cities and regions.

# Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, the dataset was prepared using Power Query.

The preparation process included:

* Removing duplicate records
* Handling missing values
* Cleaning inconsistent data
* Formatting data types
* Creating relationships
* Preparing fact and dimension tables

---

# Data Modeling

The project follows a relational data model consisting of:

* Fact Sales Table
* Customer Dimension
* Product Dimension
* Date Dimension
* Location Dimension

Relationships were established using primary and foreign keys to ensure efficient filtering and calculations.

---

# DAX Calculations

Several DAX measures were created for dynamic reporting, including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Customers
* Average Unit Price
* Previous Year Revenue
* Revenue Growth %
* Quantity Growth %
* Running Totals
* Monthly Sales

These measures allow real-time calculations based on user selections.
